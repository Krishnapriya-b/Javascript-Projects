Q: How should I clean my upholstery?

Our upholstery products have a tag located under one of the seat cushions or under the unit itself. The tag shows a universal cleaning code.


Q: What is the difference between King size and California King size beds?

California King size is 4"" longer and 4"" narrower than King size.


Q: Do you take custom orders?

Due to the large quantity of furniture we produce, we do not take custom orders. However, we do have a wide variety of styles in our dynamic product line. To find a style that will complement your home, you may want to visit our Store.
